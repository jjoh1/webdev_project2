My Favortie Musical Music

I am always discovering new music and my music tastes often change. Becuase of this, this list is far from final. It is always subject to change!

Why Musicals?

I have recently become very intrested in musicals. After getting introduced to my first musical by a friend as school, I have been captivated by their brilliance.

Here are some of my favorite songs from musicals:

  • Hamilton: An American Musical

    I am one of the very many people that have been amazed by the Hamilton musical. Almost every song keeps me entertained and it is easy to get them stuck in your head.

    My Favorite Songs From Hamilton

    • You'll Be Back

      This song strongly compels me to sing along every time. It also has some humor that makes me remember it.

    • Dear Theodosia

      This song is relatively short but still very nice. I appreciate the simplicity of the song as well.

    • Non-Stop

      Not only is Hamilton non-stop, this song is. I enjoy how this song puts down a lot of Hamilton's story while also being extremely entertaining. I also really like the many references to earlier parts of the musical.

  • Six

    After being intrigued by hearing the opening song from this musical, I discovered that I enjoy almost all of the songs included in it. While it shares many similarities to a concert rather than a musical, I still was extremely entertained by it.

    My Favorite Songs From Six

    • Get Down

      I find this song to be an invitation to start dacing. I also really like the song's defiance of societal norms.

    • All You Wanna Do

      This is one of my favorite songs to karaoke. I appreciate the strong emotions and story progression in the song.
